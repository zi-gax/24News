From Lady Gaga to Paris Hilton, these were the unforgettable looks at Coachella

Summary

Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell has expressed concern over the potential economic impact of President Trump's new tariffs, warning they could lead to higher inflation and slower growth. He indicated that the Fed would adopt a cautious approach, waiting for more clarity before making any interest rate decisions. Powell highlighted the risk of stagflation—a scenario of rising prices and weakening economic growth—that could complicate the Fed's dual mandate of maintaining stable prices and maximum employment. The uncertainty surrounding the tariffs has already affected markets, with significant stock declines observed. Powell emphasized the need for careful monitoring of the situation to guide future monetary policy decisions.

Highlights

  • Kendall Jenner embraced minimalist elegance with a butter-yellow Proenza Schouler dress, reflecting a refined shift in festival fashion.
  • Western-inspired looks were popular, with stars like Anya Taylor-Joy and Eiza González rocking cowboy boots and boho-chic outfits.
  • Charli XCX made a bold statement with a sash reading "Miss Should Be Headliner," stirring buzz and fan support.
